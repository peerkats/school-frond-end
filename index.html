About me

Hello, my name is Peer, and I am 17 years old. I am a Dutch programmer eager to learn and passionate about building applications. I was born in the Netherlands and moved to Sweden when I was one year old. I lived in Sweden until I was eight, where I learned the language very well.

In my free time, I enjoy working on side projects and going to the gym. Currently, I am studying software development at TCR in Alexandrium, Rotterdam. I live in Berkel en Rodenrijs.

Before pursuing software development, I attended the Melanchthon school, where I completed HAVO and VWO programs. However, I realized that they weren't the right fit for me, so I decided to focus on programming instead.

My journey has been filled with diverse experiences, from living in different countries to exploring various educational paths. Through it all, my passion for programming has remained a constant. I am committed to continuing my education and honing my skills to become an accomplished software developer.

I look forward to the future, where I can apply my knowledge and creativity to develop innovative applications.
